About VpasModule:

I am assuming since you are here, you probably know a thing or two about CyberArk. Like you, I was tasked to do something painfully ​repetitive in my ​environment and figured there had to be an easier, more automated way to complete my task. This is how the initial pieces of VpasModule was created, just a bunch of PowerShell files with different api calls that I used for myself to automate repetitive tasks.

Fast forward to present day, and VpasModule now has over 150 different CyberArk API commands including a handful of custom commands that have been requested for specific use cases that have been generalized

Check out VpasModule on Github and PowershellGallery! Please reach out to me any way you feel comfortable for any feedback, suggestions, etc. You can also find me on LinkedIn
